Our Mission

Artesian Transitional Housing exists to provide safe, structured, and respectful living environments that support accountability, independence, and long-term stability. We believe stable housing is a critical foundation for personal growth and success.

About Artesian Transitional Housing

Artesian Transitional Housing was established to meet a growing need in the Stuart community for dependable, recovery-focused housing options that promote responsibility and long-term independence.

We offer a range of housing options to support individuals at different stages of transition, including over 20 apartment-style units, 3–4 shared residential homes, and independent living houses for residents ready for increased autonomy.

This flexible structure allows residents to progress through different levels of independence while maintaining a supportive and stable living environment.

Housing Options

Apartment-Style Units

Shared Residential Homes

Independent Living Houses

Independent housing options for individuals ready for greater autonomy while maintaining stability.
