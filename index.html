Our Mission

Artesian Recovery exists to provide a safe, structured, and welcoming living environment for individuals transitioning into independent, substance-free lives. Our mission centers on accountability, stability, and creating a home that supports long-term success.

Our Story

Artesian Recovery was established to meet a growing need in the Stuart community for dependable, recovery-focused transitional housing. Many individuals in recovery struggle to find stable environments that promote responsibility, structure, and positive routines.

Our home was created to bridge that gap — offering a calm, respectful setting where residents can rebuild their lives, strengthen independence, and prepare for a successful future.

What We Offer

Safe & Structured Housing

Clean, quiet, and supportive living accommodations that encourage responsibility and recovery-focused routines.

Accountability & Stability

Clear expectations, consistency, and a respectful community environment that supports long-term growth.

Supportive Community

A welcoming atmosphere where residents are encouraged to build healthy habits and independence.
